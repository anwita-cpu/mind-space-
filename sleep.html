🌙 Can't Sleep

Let's quiet your mind, one step at a time.

Step 1 — Empty your mind

Whatever is keeping you awake — type it all out. Every worry, every thought, every "what if". Get it out of your head and onto the screen.

Step 2 — File it away

Your worries are real. But right now, there is nothing you can do about them. Let's put them away until tomorrow.

Step 3 — 4-7-8 breathing

This technique activates your body's natural calm response. Follow the circle.

ready

Step 4 — Drift off

Put your phone face down, close your eyes, and let the sounds carry you.

Tokyo rain sounds + lofi music. 8 hours. You don't need to do anything else tonight. Tomorrow is tomorrow. Right now, just rest. 🌙
