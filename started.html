Welcome to MindSpace 💜

A safe place for your mind. No matter what you're going through.

💬
Sage An AI companion that's available any time of day, ready to listen without judgment or trying to fix you.
📊
Mood Tracker A simple daily check-in to log how you're feeling, so you can look back and notice patterns over time.
🫙
Memory Jar A place to save the small moments that made you smile, so you have something good to come back to on harder days.
💨
The vent button A private space to type out everything you're feeling. Nothing is saved, it simply disappears the moment you close the page.
🫂
The circle An anonymous community where you can share what you're going through, and others who understand can respond.
🧘
Coping Toolkit Guided tools built for specific moments — panic attacks, anxiety, anger, sadness, and nights you can't sleep.
🚨
Crisis helplines If things ever feel like too much, real helplines are just one tap away. You are never truly alone here.
